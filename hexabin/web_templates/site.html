§ 01 · The project

Living
landmarks
for the smart city.

HexaBin is a revolutionary urban waste system that fuses IoT-driven Smart City technology with biophilic, nature-inspired design — turning a mundane chore into a civic ritual.

Deploying first in Yerevan, Armenia, every unit uses dual OAK-D depth cameras and a Gemini-powered vision model to recognise items into six material streams plus an "Empty" state — with brand-aware accuracy on Armenian products like Jermuk and BJNI.

Our bins act as visual landmarks in the urban fabric — using colour, material and small rewards to signal that the user is participating in a global movement of sustainability. We bridge the gap for citizens currently unmotivated to sort.

§ 02 · The modular system

Six material streams,
one elevated language of colour.

Each module is visually intuitive yet architecturally sophisticated — premium tones reflecting urban civic space, not the trash room.

01 / 06

Paper

Warm Cellulose

Newspapers, cardboard, office paper, packaging. Sophisticated take on traditional fiber.

02 / 06

Aluminum

Brushed Metal

Beverage cans, foil, metal containers. Industrial circularity, detected by depth + vision.

03 / 06

Organic

Deep Biophilic

Food scraps, biodegradable packaging, garden waste — composted for circular sustainability.

04 / 06

Glass

Crystal Teal

Bottles, jars, glass containers — identified by their unique transparency signatures.

05 / 06

Plastic

Synthetic Tone

PET, HDPE, films. Classified by brand-aware AI trained on local Armenian products.

06 / 06

Other

Dynamic Gradient

The "brain" of the system. Adapts based on city-wide data, special collection, system signals.
